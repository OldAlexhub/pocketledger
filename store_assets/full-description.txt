PocketLedger — Private Budget Clarity

Take control of your spending without giving up your privacy. PocketLedger is a completely offline budget organization app that runs entirely on your phone. No account. No bank connection. No cloud sync. No data leaves your device.

WHAT YOU CAN DO

Import bank statements — Copy and paste transaction text from your bank's website, or select a text or CSV file from your device. PocketLedger reads and classifies it locally using a built-in rules engine.

Understand your spending — See your income, spending, and balance at a glance. Break down spending by category and compare months side by side.

Track transactions — Add income and expenses manually. Filter, search, and edit transactions by category, type, or merchant. Reclassify categories to match your habits.

Plan your budget — Set your monthly income, savings goal, fixed bills, and category budgets. See a clear budget breakdown: Income minus fixed bills minus spending equals what you actually have available.

Detect subscriptions — PocketLedger automatically identifies recurring charges from your imported statements. See monthly and yearly costs side by side and decide which to keep, ignore, or review.

Find savings potential — Based on your imported spending patterns, PocketLedger estimates how much you could potentially save by adjusting flexible categories. Three scenarios: Conservative, Balanced, and Aggressive.

Export reports — Generate a monthly spending summary as an HTML report. Export all transactions as a CSV spreadsheet. Both are shared through Android's standard sharing, so you control where they go.

Optional App Lock — Protect your budget data with biometric or PIN authentication. PocketLedger does not store or transmit any biometric data. Authentication is handled entirely by your Android device.

COMPLETELY PRIVATE

- No account required
- No bank connection
- No internet required
- No data uploaded or transmitted
- Statement imports processed locally on your device
- Transactions stored only on your device
- No analytics, no advertising, no tracking

IMPORTANT NOTES

PocketLedger is a personal budget organization tool. It is not a bank, financial advisor, tax service, investment platform, or credit service. All calculations and estimates are based on the data you enter or import. This app does not provide financial advice.

Statement import works best with plain text or CSV exports from your bank's website. Full PDF extraction is not available in this version.
