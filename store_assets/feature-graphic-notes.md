# Feature Graphic Notes — PocketLedger

For Google Play feature graphic (1024 x 500 px).

---

## Design Direction

**Background:** Deep navy blue (#1B4F72) — solid or with a subtle gradient to #154360

**Layout:** Centered content with the logo above the headline

---

## Content

**App Logo:** Centered, approximately 120px tall — use pocketledger/assets/logo.png

**Primary Headline:**
```
PocketLedger
```
Font: Bold, white, approximately 64px

**Subheadline:**
```
Private Budget Clarity
```
Font: Regular or medium weight, white at 75% opacity, approximately 32px

**Tagline (smaller, below subheadline):**
```
Import statements. Detect subscriptions. Find savings potential.
```
Font: Light weight, white at 55% opacity, approximately 20px

---

## Visual Elements (optional)

Small pill badges arranged horizontally near the bottom:
- 🔒 Local Only
- 📊 Offline
- 💰 No Account

Background texture: Subtle diagonal lines or soft geometric grid overlay at 5% opacity

---

## Color Reference

| Element | Color |
|---|---|
| Background | #1B4F72 |
| Background gradient end | #154360 |
| Logo | White or brand colors |
| Headline text | #FFFFFF |
| Subheadline text | rgba(255,255,255,0.75) |
| Tagline text | rgba(255,255,255,0.55) |
| Badge background | rgba(255,255,255,0.15) |
| Badge text | rgba(255,255,255,0.9) |

---

## Notes

- Keep it minimal and professional
- No stock photos of people or money
- Avoid financial imagery that implies investment advice
- The design should convey privacy, clarity, and trust
