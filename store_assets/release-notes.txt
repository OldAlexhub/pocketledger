Version 1.0.0

Initial release of PocketLedger.

- Dashboard with monthly income, spending, and balance overview
- Transaction management: add, edit, delete, and categorize
- Statement import via file picker or paste text
- Local transaction classification engine with 19 categories
- Budget planner with income, savings goal, and fixed bills
- Subscription detection from imported statements
- Spending insights and anomaly detection
- Savings potential calculator with three scenarios
- Monthly reports and CSV export
- Optional App Lock with biometric or device PIN
- Full offline operation — no internet required
- No account, no backend, no data collection
