# Privacy Policy — PocketLedger

**Last updated:** May 2026

---

## Overview

PocketLedger is a private, offline personal budget organization app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains exactly how PocketLedger handles your data.

---

## No Account Required

PocketLedger does not require you to create an account. There is no sign-up, no login, and no user profile stored on any server.

---

## No Data Collection

PocketLedger does not collect, store, or process any personal data on external servers.

- No financial data is transmitted to any server or third party.
- No analytics or telemetry data is collected.
- No advertising identifiers are used.
- No personal information is stored outside of your device.

---

## Local-Only Data Storage

All data you enter or import into PocketLedger — including transactions, statements, budgets, and settings — is stored **only on your device** using Android's local storage.

- Transactions are stored locally on the device.
- Statement imports are processed locally on the device.
- Budget settings and preferences are stored locally on the device.
- No data is synchronized to any cloud service.

---

## Statement Imports

When you import a bank statement or paste statement text:

- The file or text is processed entirely on your device.
- No file contents are transmitted to any server.
- No statement data leaves your device.
- Imported transactions are stored only in the app's local storage.
- You may delete imported statements at any time from the Import screen.

---

## App Lock

PocketLedger includes an optional App Lock feature:

- App Lock is entirely optional and disabled by default.
- When enabled, the app uses Android's built-in biometric or device PIN authentication.
- **PocketLedger does not collect, store, or transmit biometric data.**
- All biometric and screen lock authentication is handled exclusively by the Android operating system on your device.
- PocketLedger does not have access to your biometric data.

---

## Exported Files

- When you export a report or CSV file, the export is handled through Android's standard sharing mechanism.
- You control where exported files are sent.
- PocketLedger does not access or transmit exported files.

---

## Data Deletion

You can permanently delete all app data at any time:

1. Open PocketLedger
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Tap **Reset All Data**

This deletes all transactions, statements, budgets, bills, subscriptions, insights, and settings from your device.

Uninstalling the app also removes all locally stored data.

---

## Internet Permission

PocketLedger does **not** require or use an internet connection for any core functionality. The app is designed to work fully offline.

---

## What This App Is Not

PocketLedger is a personal budget organization tool. It is NOT:

- A bank or financial institution
- A financial advisor
- Tax software
- Investment software
- A credit or loan service
- Connected to any bank or financial account

PocketLedger does not access, connect to, or communicate with any bank, financial institution, or external data source.

---

## No Third-Party Sharing

PocketLedger does not share, sell, or disclose any user data to third parties because no user data is ever collected or stored outside your device.

---

## Children's Privacy

PocketLedger does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since no data is collected, no special risk exists.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the updated version will be included in future app updates. Since no data leaves your device, changes to this policy do not affect your existing data.
